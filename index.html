About

Mr. James Alexander Wilson is a visionary leader and financial strategist with over 15 years of experience in international investments and wealth management. As the Founder and CEO of MicroYield International Investment, he has pioneered innovative micro-investment solutions that have democratized access to global financial markets.

Under his leadership, MicroYield has grown from a startup to a multinational investment firm with operations in 12 countries and assets under management exceeding $2.5 billion.

Mr. Wilson's unique approach combines cutting-edge financial technology with traditional investment wisdom, creating opportunities for investors at all wealth levels.
